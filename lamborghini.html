Lamborghini Urus

Details:

The Lamborghini Urus SE is a plug-in hybrid sports SUV with a 4-liter V8 engine, electric motors, and all-wheel drive. It has a top speed of 312 kmph and can go from 0 to 100 kmph in 3.4 seconds.

Engine:

  • 3996 cc, 8 cylinders in V shape, 4 valves/cylinder, DOHC
  • 789 bhp @ 6000 rpm
  • 800 Nm @ 2250 rpm
  • 25.9 kWh lithium ion battery

    • Transmission:

  • Automatic (DCT) - 8 Gears, Paddle Shift, Sport Mode

    • Performance: 0-100 kmph in 3.4 seconds, Top speed of 312 kmph, and Electric range of 59 km.

    Features:

  • Revised and sleeker LED headlamps
  • New alloy wheel design
  • New spoiler
  • Fresh tail lamp elements
  • New dashboard panels
  • New air vents design
  • 12.3-inch centre-stacked touchscreen infotainment system
  • Regenerative braking
  • Idle start/stop

    • Safety: Anti-lock braking system (ABS), Hill hold control, and Child safety lock.

    Other features: Rain sensing wipers, Automatic headlamps, Cruise control, and Paddle shifters.
