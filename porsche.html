911 Carrera 2025

Details:

The 2025 Porsche 911 Carrera continues the legacy of the iconic sports car with notable enhancements in performance and technology.

Engine and Performance:

  • Engine: 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six
  • Power: 388 horsepower
  • Torque: 331 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-speed PDK dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 3.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 183 mph
    • These figures represent a slight increase over the previous model, enhancing acceleration and overall performance.
    Design and Features: The 2025 Carrera maintains its classic silhouette with subtle refinements. The interior boasts a 10.9-inch touchscreen with the latest Porsche Communication Management system, offering seamless connectivity and navigation. Advanced driver-assistance features, including adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assist, are available to enhance safety and convenience.
    Pricing: The starting price for the 2025 Porsche 911 Carrera is approximately $122,095. This positions it competitively within the luxury sports car market, offering a blend of performance, heritage, and modern amenities.
    For those seeking enhanced performance, the Carrera S variant offers increased horsepower and additional features, catering to driving enthusiasts desiring a more dynamic experience. In summary, the 2025 Porsche 911 Carrera upholds the model's esteemed reputation, delivering a harmonious balance of performance, luxury, and cutting-edge technology.
